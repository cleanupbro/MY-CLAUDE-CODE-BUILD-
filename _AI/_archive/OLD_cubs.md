# PART 3: SERVICE DEFINITIONS

## 3.1 The Four Cleaning Missions

| Service | Mission | Objective |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| **General Clean** | Maintenance | Keep a reasonably clean home tidy and hygienic |
| **Deep Clean** | Restoration | Reset the home to near-new condition |
| **End-of-Lease** | Compliance | Pass real estate inspection, get bond back |
| **Airbnb Turnover** | Guest-Ready | Fast, reliable, 5-star standard between guests |

**Simple version:**
- General clean = **maintain**
- Deep clean = **reset**
- End-of-lease / bond back = **pass inspection**
- Airbnb turnover = **guest-ready**

One keeps things nice. One fixes neglect. One protects your money. One protects your reviews.

---

## 3.2 General Clean (Maintenance)

### Purpose
Keep a reasonably clean home tidy and hygienic. For homes that are already in decent shape and cleaned regularly.

### Scope
- Surfaces wiped (not scrubbed)
- Floors vacuumed and mopped
- Bathrooms and kitchens cleaned to everyday standard
- Bins emptied
- General tidying

### Exclusions
- No heavy scrubbing
- No moving furniture
- No attacking long-term buildup
- No inside appliances
- No detailed grout work

### Ideal For
- Clients with ongoing weekly/fortnightly bookings
- Homes already maintained to reasonable standard
- Time-poor professionals who want to maintain, not restore

---

## 3.3 Deep Clean (Restoration) ‚≠ê FLAGSHIP SERVICE

### Purpose
Reset the home to near-new condition. A one-off intensive clean that goes far beyond regular cleaning ‚Äî targeting built-up grime, hidden dust, and neglected areas.

### The Deep Clean Difference
```
A regular clean MAINTAINS cleanliness.
A deep clean RESTORES it.
```

### Scope ‚Äî Everything in General Clean, PLUS:

**All Rooms:**
- Surfaces SCRUBBED rather than wiped
- Dust removed from skirting boards, door frames, window sills, light switches, power points, vents
- Cobwebs cleared
- Furniture wiped down
- Light furniture moved to clean underneath and behind (where safe/possible)
- Floors vacuumed and mopped thoroughly, edges included

**Kitchen:**
- Appliances cleaned externally (internally if requested ‚Äî add-on)
- Benchtops, splashbacks, cupboard doors, handles, kickboards DEGREASED
- Sink descaled and polished
- Stovetops scrubbed to remove baked-on residue
- Built-up grease and food residue properly treated (not masked)

**Bathroom:**
- Tiles and grout SCRUBBED
- Shower screens DESCALED
- Toilets deep sanitised
- Sinks, taps, mirrors, fittings polished
- Soap scum, mould residue, hard-water marks TREATED
- Cabinets and storage fronts wiped down

**Bedrooms & Living Areas:**
- Beds, tables, chairs, visible furniture cleaned thoroughly
- Skirting boards and corners get detailed attention
- Dust removed from behind and under furniture (where accessible)

**Windows (Internal):**
- Internal glass and frames cleaned
- Tracks and sills wiped and detailed (if included in booking)

### Ideal For
- Properties not professionally cleaned in a while
- Preparing for guests or inspection
- STARTING ongoing regular cleaning (deep clean first, then maintain)
- Anyone wanting a noticeably higher standard than maintenance cleaning

### Upsell Path
**"Deep Clean Plus"** (+$50‚Äì80):
- Inside fridge
- Inside oven
- Heavy grout or mould treatment

### Sales Psychology
Position as a "reset clean" ‚Äî clients accept the higher price once they understand it reduces future costs and makes ongoing cleans faster.

## 3.4 End-of-Lease / Bond Back Clean (Compliance)

### Purpose
Meet real estate and property manager standards. Pass inspection and get the bond back. This is NOT about comfort ‚Äî it's about compliance.

### Scope ‚Äî Checklist-Driven, Inspection-Focused

**Everything cleaned to inspection level:**
- Inside cupboards
- Inside oven
- Internal windows (glass, frames, tracks, sills)
- Skirting boards
- Walls spot-cleaned
- Bathrooms fully descaled
- Floors detailed
- Light fittings (dead insects removed)
- Exhaust fans
- Rangehood and filters

**The goal is simple:** Pass inspection and get the bond back.

### Key Differentiator from Deep Clean
- Follows a **checklist**, not personal preference
- Includes more areas (inside cupboards, inside oven, walls)
- Designed for **agent scrutiny**, not homeowner satisfaction
- Higher liability = higher price

### Bond Back Guarantee (72-Hour Window)
```
Clean Up Bros offers a 100% Bond Back Guarantee.

If your real estate agent identifies any cleaning issues within 72 HOURS 
of service completion, forward the Agent's Condition Report and photos 
to us. We will return to rectify issues that fall within our original 
scope of works at no additional charge.

Claims made after 72 hours will be assessed on a case-by-case basis.
```

**Why 72 Hours (Not 24):**
- Real estate agents typically take 2‚Äì3 business days to inspect
- 24-hour windows may be deemed "Unfair Contract Terms" under Australian Consumer Law (ACL)
- 72 hours is industry standard and legally defensible

### Problem

### Resolution Workflow
1. **Verify** ‚Äî Check bank for cleared funds (match amount + reference)
2. **Navigate** ‚Äî Square Dashboard ‚Üí Invoices

4. **Method** ‚Äî Select **"Other"** (NOT Card)
5. **Memo** ‚Äî `Paid via Direct Deposit on [DATE] - Ref: [REF]`
6. **Save** ‚Äî Stops automated reminders
